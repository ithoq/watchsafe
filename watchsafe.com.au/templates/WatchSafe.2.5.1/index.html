Learn More!

WatchSafe makes it easy to monitor your childs driving habits and much more

  • Driver Grading

    WatchSafe monitors your childs driving habits and provides a driver score for each journey. This is then easily visible online.

  • Locate Vehicles

    Easily locate any of your tracked vehicles via your online dashboard. View their current speed and last alert generated.

  • WatchZones

    You set the boundries and we help enforce them. Easily set an area on a map and we can tell you if and when a your child enters or exits.

  • Engine Health

    Frequently Young drivers pay little attention to Engine Warning lights or high engine temperatures. WatchSafe can tell if this occurs and will alert you

  • Journey History

    Easily view your childs journey history in your dashboard. Look at each individual journey on a map to see when and where they have been.

  • Speed Limit

    P1 Drivers are limited to 90km/h. Watchsafe lets you set a maximum speed limit. If its broken you are alerted.

Teen drivers have the highest fatal crash risk of any age group.

Young Drivers (17-25 years) represent one-quarter of all Australian road deaths, but are only 10-15% of the licensed driver population.
